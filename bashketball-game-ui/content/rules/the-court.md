The court is a hex grid, 5 rows (0–4) by 14 columns (0–13). Coordinates are written as (row, column).

## Baskets

Two Basket hexes (orange) are located at opposite ends of the court:

- **Left Basket:** (2,0)
- **Right Basket:** (2,13)

## Center Circle

The Center Circle (purple) occupies 7 hexes at mid-court:
- (1,7), (2,6), (2,7), (2,8), (3,6), (3,7), and (4,7)

During setup, one player from each team must be placed in the Center Circle for the Tip-Off.

## Scoring Zones

**2-Point Zone:** Shots from columns 0–3 (left side) or columns 10–13 (right side) score 2 points. These are the first 4 columns from each basket.

**3-Point Zone (yellow):** Shots from columns 4–9 (center court) score 3 points. The yellow hexes mark the boundary of the 3-point line.

## Court Halves

- **Left Half:** Columns 0–6
- **Right Half:** Columns 7–13

During setup, players (other than those in the Center Circle) must be placed in their own half of the court.
