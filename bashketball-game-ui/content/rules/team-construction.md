A team consists of **Player Cards** and **Deck Cards**.

## Requirements

- **Players:** A team must have 4-6 Player Cards
- **Deck Size:** Minimally, Deck Cards equal to the sum of the Player deck sizes
- **Card Copies:** A Deck can only have up to four copies of a given Deck Card
- **Standard Actions:** You can include an unlimited number of Standard Action cards in your deck

## Player Deck Requirements

Player cards specify a minimum number of cards that must be in your deck to include the player on your team. When building your deck, ensure you meet the minimum requirements for all players you wish to include.
