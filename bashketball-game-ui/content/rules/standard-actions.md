---
title: "Standard Actions"
description: "The three universal actions available to all teams"
order: 5
---

There are 3 Standard Action Cards: **Shoot / Block**, **Pass / Steal**, and **Screen / Check**. At any point during a turn, a player can discard 2 cards from their hand and spend an action to perform any of the Standard Actions.

| Action | Offense | Defense |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Shoot / Block** | Attempt Shot within 7⬡ | Force opponent to shoot or exhaust |
| **Pass / Steal** | Attempt Pass within 6⬡ | Attempt to steal the ball |
| **Screen / Check** | Screen defender, possible injury | Check opponent, possible injury |

## Shoot / Block

| Side | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Offense** | Ball carrier within 7⬡ of basket attempts Shot, ↷ |
| **Defense** | Your player adjacent to opponent within 4⬡ of basket: Opponent must attempt Shot or ↷ and skip next unexhaust. You ↷ |

## Pass / Steal

| Side | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Offense** | Ball carrier attempts Pass to teammate within 6⬡, ↷ |
| **Defense** | Engage ball carrier within 2⬡. Attempt Defend-2. SM defender ignores size Disadvantage. ✓ You take the ball. ✗ You ↷ |

## Screen / Check

| Side | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Offense** | Engage defender within 2⬡. Attempt Defend-1. ✓ Both ↷; opponent skips next unexhaust. ✗ You ↷. ✓+2 Attempt Injure. ✗-3 Attempt Injure on you |
| **Defense** | Engage opponent within 2⬡. Attempt Defend-1. ✓ Both ↷; opponent skips next unexhaust. ✗ You ↷. ✓+2 Attempt Injure. ✗-3 Attempt Injure on you |

## Exhaust

Some actions may cause a Team Player to become Exhausted. Cards may say they cause a player to be exhausted. An exhausted player does not exert ZoC. Some Team Assets may be exhausted after using their ability. During a player's upkeep phase, exhausted cards become un-exhausted.
