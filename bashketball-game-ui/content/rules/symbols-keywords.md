## Icons

| Symbol | Name | Meaning |
|--------|------|---------|
| ⬡ | Hex | Unit of distance on the court. "Within 4⬡" means 4 or fewer hexes away. |
| ↷ | Exhaust | Turn the player card sideways. An exhausted player cannot act until they unexhaust. |
| ✓ | Success | Effect applies when the action succeeds. |
| ✗ | Failure | Effect applies when the action fails. |
| ✓+N | Success by N+ | Effect applies when fate exceeds difficulty by N or more. |

## Keywords

**Ball carrier** — Your player currently holding the ball.

**Engage X⬡** — Move your player so they are adjacent to a target opponent who is within X⬡ of your player's starting position. ZoC movement costs count toward this distance—if moving through enemy ZoC would cost more than X⬡ total movement, you cannot engage that target.

**Defend** / **Defend-N** — Attempt a Defensive Action. When written as "Defend-N" (e.g., Defend-1, Defend-2), the number indicates a penalty to your Defense skill for this action. Compare your player's DEF (minus any penalty) + fate value against the difficulty.

**Shot** / **Shot-N** — Attempt to score. When written as "Shot-N" (e.g., Shot-1, Shot-2), the number indicates a penalty to your Shot skill for this action. Compare shooter's SHT (minus any penalty) + fate value against the shot difficulty (based on distance and defenders).

**Pass** / **Pass-N** — Attempt to move the ball to a teammate. When written as "Pass-N" (e.g., Pass-1, Pass-2), the number indicates a penalty to your Pass skill for this action. Compare passer's PSS (minus any penalty) + fate value against the pass difficulty.

**Injure** — Attempt to injure an opponent. An injured player is removed from the game.

**Skip next unexhaust** — The affected player remains exhausted during the next unexhaust phase.

**Retain** — Do not discard this card at the end of your turn.
